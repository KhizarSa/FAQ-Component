# Front-end Style Guide

## Layout

The designs were created to the following widths:

- Mobile: 375px
- Desktop: 1440px

## Colors

### Primary

#### Text

- Very dark desaturated blue: hsl(238, 29%, 16%) OR #1d1e35
- Soft red: hsl(14, 88%, 65%) OR #f47c57

#### Gradient

Background gradient:

- Soft violet: hsl(273, 75%, 66%)
- Soft blue: hsl(240, 73%, 65%)

### Neutral

#### Text

- Very dark grayish blue: hsl(237, 12%, 33%) OR #4a4b5e
- Dark grayish blue: hsl(240, 6%, 50%) OR #787887

#### Dividers

- Light grayish blue: hsl(240, 5%, 91%)

## Typography

### Body Copy

- Font size: 12px

### Font

- Family: [Kumbh Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Kumbh+Sans)
- Weights: 400, 700
